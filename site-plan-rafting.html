Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our rafting website is to provide information about our rafting business, including details about our services, upcoming trips, safety measures, and how customers can book their rafting adventures online.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, families, and groups of friends looking for an exciting outdoor experience. They may have varying levels of experience with rafting, from beginners to experienced rafters.

Scenario Questions:

  • Q1: Are you interested in experiencing the thrill of white-water rafting?
  • A1: Yes, I've always wanted to try white-water rafting and I'm looking for a reputable company to guide me through the experience.
  • Q2: Are you planning a group outing or family adventure?
  • A2: Yes, I'm organizing a trip for my friends/family, and I'm looking for an activity that everyone can enjoy together.
  • Q3: Do you have previous rafting experience?
  • A3: Yes, I've been rafting before and I'm seeking more challenging rapids and new rivers to explore.
